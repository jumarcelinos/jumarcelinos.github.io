Juliana Marcelino

Visual Designer, Branding, Graphic Design — Denmark

My journey began in corporate communication in Brazil and evolved into branding and graphic design after moving to Denmark. Over the years, I’ve partnered with startups, nonprofits, and purpose-led businesses—helping them bring their brand identities to life. Today, as U/UX Designer, I focus on designing intuitive, scalable digital interfaces that bridge the gap between brand identity and product functionality.
